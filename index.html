Research Interests
My scholarship centers on understanding and predicting variability of the atmosphere, cryosphere, and ocean across a variety of time and spatial scales, with particular emphases on the subseasonal scale and teleconnections from the tropics to high latitudes. As our numerical weather and climate prediction understanding and methods continue to improve, my goal is to position my group at the cutting edge of our science. I achieve that goal by applying advanced statistical techniques to a combination of observations (including atmospheric and ocean reanalyses), theory and model output. If you are interested in working with me, please get in touch!
